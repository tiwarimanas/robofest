Anti Sleep Alarm System Project

From..

Ayan, Sobiya, and Yashraj - Class 7

Application

The Anti Sleep Alarm System project is designed to prevent accidents caused by driver drowsiness or fatigue. An IR sensor, mounted on the driver's glasses, continuously monitors the driver's eye movements. If the sensor detects the driver closing their eyes for an extended period, indicating drowsiness or sleepiness, the system activates an alarm to alert the driver and prompts them to regain focus or pull over safely.

System Behind

The project utilizes an IR sensor mounted on the driver's glasses to detect eye movements. The sensor sends data to an Arduino or similar microcontroller, which processes the information in real-time. If the microcontroller detects prolonged eye closure, it triggers an alarm system to alert the driver. This system helps prevent accidents caused by driver drowsiness or fatigue, promoting road safety and reducing the risk of collisions.
