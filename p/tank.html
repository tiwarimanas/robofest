Advanced Water Tank Project

From..

Divya, Om, and Gautam - Class 7

Application

The Advanced Water Tank project enhances water management efficiency by utilizing an ultrasonic sensor to monitor the water level in the tank. Based on the water level data, the system automatically controls a motor to draw water from the tank when needed. This automated approach ensures optimal water usage and helps prevent overflows or shortages, making it an essential tool for sustainable water conservation.

System Behind

The project consists of an ultrasonic sensor installed at the top of the water tank to measure the distance to the water surface. This sensor sends data to an Arduino or similar microcontroller, which processes the information and determines the water level. Based on predefined thresholds, the microcontroller activates a motor to draw water from the tank when the level is low. This automated system ensures efficient water management and helps maintain a consistent water supply for various applications.
