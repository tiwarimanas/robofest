Application

The Security Alarm project provides a simple yet effective solution for detecting unauthorized entry or theft. Equipped with sensors, the alarm system can detect when someone enters a restricted area or tries to tamper with valuables. Upon detection, the system activates a buzzer to alert residents or security personnel, deterring potential intruders and enhancing overall security measures.

System Behind

The project incorporates sensors placed strategically to detect motion or unauthorized entry. When a sensor is triggered, it sends a signal to a microcontroller (such as Arduino), which in turn activates a buzzer to emit an audible alarm. This system effectively alerts individuals within the vicinity to the presence of a potential intruder, enabling timely response and preventing theft or unauthorized access.
