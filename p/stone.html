Stone Paper Scissors Project

From..

Khushi - Class 7

Application

The Stone Paper Scissors project brings the classic game to life using innovative technology. Utilizing an ultrasonic sensor to detect the presence of a person, the system randomly activates one of three servo motors, each attached to a hand-cutout representing stone, paper, or scissors. This interactive project adds a fun and engaging twist to the traditional game, providing entertainment for players of all ages.

System Behind

The project employs an ultrasonic sensor to detect the presence of a person within its range. When a person is detected, the system activates one of three servo motors randomly, simulating the player's choice of stone, paper, or scissors. This random selection adds an element of surprise to the game, enhancing the overall experience for players. The Stone Paper Scissors project showcases the potential of using sensors and actuators to create interactive and engaging applications.
