From..

Dev and Deep - Class 9

Application

The Smoke Alarm Sensor project aims to enhance fire safety by detecting smoke in its early stages. Utilizing a smoke sensor, the system emits a loud beep when smoke is detected, alerting occupants of potential fire hazards. This project is crucial for homes, offices, and public buildings, providing an effective means of early warning and evacuation in the event of a fire emergency.

System Behind

The project relies on a smoke sensor to detect the presence of smoke particles in the air. When smoke is detected, the sensor sends a signal to a microcontroller, such as Arduino, which triggers a buzzer to emit a loud beep. This audible alarm alerts occupants to the presence of smoke, allowing them to take immediate action to mitigate fire risks. The Smoke Alarm Sensor project demonstrates the importance of early detection and intervention in fire safety protocols.
