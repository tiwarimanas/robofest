Application

The Automated Air Cooler project enhances user comfort by automating the operation of an air cooler based on environmental conditions. Equipped with sensors to measure temperature and humidity, the system provides automated climate control, ensuring optimal cooling performance for indoor environments.

System Behind

The project utilizes sensors such as temperature and humidity sensors to monitor environmental conditions. These sensors provide real-time data to an Arduino or similar microcontroller, which processes the information to automate the operation of the air cooler. By integrating sensors and microcontrollers, the system offers a convenient and efficient solution for automated climate control, enhancing user comfort in indoor spaces.
