Fire Fighting Car Project

From..

Agrima, Mahi, Anirudh - Class 8

Application

The Fire Fighting Car project provides an efficient solution for extinguishing fires autonomously. Equipped with a flame sensor, the car can detect flames and respond by dispensing water or fire retardants to suppress the fire. This project demonstrates the potential of robotics and sensor technology in enhancing firefighting capabilities and improving response times in emergency situations.

System Behind

The project utilizes a flame sensor to detect the presence of flames. When flames are detected, the sensor sends a signal to a microcontroller (such as Arduino), which activates a water pump or sprayer mechanism to dispense water or fire retardants. This automated system enables the fire-fighting car to quickly and effectively respond to fire outbreaks, mitigating potential damage and protecting lives and property.
