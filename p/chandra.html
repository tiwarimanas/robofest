Chandrayaan 3 Launch Model

From..

Bhawarth, Mihir, and Piyush - Class 6

Application

The Chandrayaan 3 Launch Model project simulates the countdown and launch sequence of the Chandrayaan 3 mission. Utilizing a timer, buzzer, and LCD display, the project accurately counts down from 10 seconds to simulate the final moments before liftoff. Additionally, a rotating motor is used to demonstrate the lift-off of the rocket model, providing a visual representation of the launch sequence.

System Behind

The project incorporates a timer circuit with a buzzer and LCD display to create the countdown sequence. The timer starts from 10 seconds and decrements in real-time until liftoff. A rotating motor is connected to a model rocket, which lifts off at the end of the countdown, simulating the launch of Chandrayaan 3. This integration of electronics and mechanical components provides an engaging and educational experience, allowing students to learn about space exploration and mission planning.
