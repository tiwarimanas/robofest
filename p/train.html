Application

The Anti-Collision Train System project is designed to enhance railway safety by utilizing ultrasonic sensors to detect approaching trains and alerting individuals near the tracks. When a train is detected, the system activates a buzzer to alert pedestrians or workers near the railway tracks, preventing potential collisions and ensuring their safety.

System Behind

The project incorporates ultrasonic sensors placed along the railway tracks to detect approaching trains. When a train is detected within a certain range, the sensor sends a signal to a microcontroller (such as Arduino), which activates a buzzer to emit an audible warning. This system effectively alerts individuals near the tracks to the presence of an oncoming train, reducing the risk of accidents and improving overall railway safety.
