Line Following Car Project

From..

Aniket Sharma, Nikhil Tiwari and Shourya Mishra - Class 9

Application

The Line Following Car project introduces an innovative approach to automation. By utilizing infrared sensors and a microcontroller, the car is programmed to follow a predefined path marked by a line on the ground. This technology finds applications in industries such as warehousing, transportation, and logistics, where autonomous navigation is essential for efficiency and safety.

System Behind

The core of the Line Following Car project lies in the integration of infrared sensors with a microcontroller, such as Arduino. These sensors detect the contrast between the line and the surrounding surface, allowing the microcontroller to make real-time adjustments to the car's direction. By employing principles of feedback control, the car can navigate along the path with precision and reliability, showcasing the potential of automation in various practical scenarios.
