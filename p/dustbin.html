Smart Dustbin Project

From..

Yash - Class 6

Application

The Smart Dustbin project aims to improve waste management by automating the process of opening the lid when a person approaches. Utilizing a servo motor and an ultrasonic sensor, the system detects the presence of a person in front of the dustbin and opens the lid for a predefined duration of 3 seconds. This innovative solution promotes cleanliness and convenience, making waste disposal more efficient and hygienic.

System Behind

The project employs an ultrasonic sensor to detect the distance to the nearest object in front of the dustbin. When a person is detected within a certain range, the microcontroller triggers a servo motor to open the lid of the dustbin for 3 seconds. This automatic lid opening mechanism facilitates hands-free waste disposal, promoting cleanliness and hygiene. The Smart Dustbin project demonstrates the potential of technology to enhance everyday tasks and improve quality of life.
