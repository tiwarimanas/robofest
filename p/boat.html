Application

The Smart Board project utilizes ultrasonic sensors to detect obstacles in its path and maneuver accordingly to avoid collisions. Whether used as a robotic platform or a smart cart, this project demonstrates autonomous navigation capabilities by sensing the environment and adjusting its course to prevent accidents or obstructions. This project showcases the potential of sensor-based technologies in creating safer and more efficient autonomous systems.

System Behind

The project incorporates ultrasonic sensors mounted on the smart board to detect objects or obstacles in its vicinity. Using data from these sensors, a microcontroller (such as Arduino) processes the information and controls the movement of the board. When an obstacle is detected, the microcontroller triggers appropriate motor actions to navigate the board away from the obstruction, ensuring smooth and safe traversal in various environments.
