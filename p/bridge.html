Application

The Smart Bridge project is designed to mitigate the impact of floods by utilizing innovative technology. By incorporating an Arduino Uno microcontroller and a moisture sensor, the system can detect rising water levels and autonomously lift the bridge slightly above, ensuring the safety of commuters and preventing damage to infrastructure.

System Behind

The heart of the Smart Bridge project is the Arduino Uno microcontroller, programmed to interact with the moisture sensor. When the sensor detects a significant rise in water level indicative of flooding, the microcontroller activates the lifting mechanism, raising the bridge to a safe height. This system operates autonomously, providing a reliable and efficient solution to address flood-related challenges in infrastructure management
